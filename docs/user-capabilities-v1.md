# What Users Can Do — Interactive Ideas V1
**User-Facing Capabilities After 4-Week Implementation**

## Getting Started

### Create Your Venture
- Choose your project type (Venture is primary in V1)
- Select your persona character (2 options: male or female sprite)
- Get randomly assigned a Super Boss (3 options in V1)
- Your venture begins at Stage 1 with the world in darkness

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

### Navigate the World Map
- View your adventure map with 8 distinct biomes:
  1. Village (Stage 1)
  2. Forest (Stage 2)
  3. Arena (Stage 3)
  4. Artisan's Quarter (Stage 4)
  5. Mine (Stage 5)
  6. Harbour (Stage 6)
  7. Crossroads (Stage 7)
  8. Capital (Stage 8)

- Watch your persona sprite walk along the snake path
- See the world gradually brighten as you complete stages (0% → 100%)
- Scroll horizontally through the map as you progress

### Complete Checkpoints
Each stage has 4-5 checkpoint tasks. For each checkpoint you can:

1. **View the Task**
   - Read the checkpoint challenge
   - See what tool is required (11 options available)
   - Understand the completion criteria

2. **Submit Your Work**
   - Use one of 11 tools to complete the task:
     - Write (text editor)
     - Table (spreadsheet)
     - Map/Canvas (visual mapping)
     - Survey (create surveys)
     - Poll (quick polls)
     - Link (share URLs)
     - Upload (files/images)
     - OAuth (connect accounts)
     - Self-report (manual entry)
     - Journal (reflective writing)
     - Kanban (task boards)
     - Calendar (scheduling)
   
   - Must include a contribution:
     - Text (minimum 50 words)
     - Audio recording
     - Video
     - Image
     - File upload

3. **Get AI Quality Feedback**
   - Receive instant AI evaluation on 4 dimensions:
     - Completeness (0-3)
     - Specificity (0-3)
     - Evidence (0-3)
     - Originality (0-3)
   - See your Quality Tier: Low (0-4), Standard (5-8), or High (9-12)
   - Track your Valuation Score (user-facing metric)

4. **Watch Checkpoint Animations**
   - Standard completion: 1.5-2.5 second animation
   - Gold completion (high quality): 2.5-3.5 second animation
   - 6 unique animation patterns across stages:
     - Stage 1: Seal Break
     - Stage 2: Rune Inscription
     - Stage 3: Beacon Lighting
     - Stage 4: Bridge Repair
     - Stage 5: Compass Calibration
     - Stage 6: Ward Placement
     - Stage 7: Beacon Lighting
     - Stage 8: Seal Break
   - Skip animations after 0.5 seconds if desired

5. **Earn Rewards**
   - Gain XP for each checkpoint completed
   - More XP for higher quality submissions
   - Gold checkpoints trigger community notifications

## Progression System

### Level Up
- Earn XP from checkpoint completions
- Level up through 40 levels across 4 phases:
  - Novice (Levels 1-10)
  - Apprentice (Levels 11-20)
  - Journeyman (Levels 21-30)
  - Expert (Levels 31-40)
- Watch level-up animations:
  - Screen edge burst
  - Level counter spin
  - New title reveal
  - Phase transition effects (at boundaries)
  - New tool/ability cards
  - Total: 2 seconds, skippable after 0.5s

### Earn Badges
- Unlock achievement badges for milestones
- 5 rarity tiers with different animations:
  - Common
  - Uncommon
  - Rare
  - Epic
  - Legendary (full-screen gold particle burst)
- Badge award sequence:
  - Interrupt flash
  - Badge materializes with bounce
  - Reveal card with details
  - Auto-dismiss after 4 seconds (except Legendary)

### Track Your Progress
View real-time stats in the HUD:
- Current XP and progress to next level
- Current level and phase
- Stage name and icon
- Checkpoint progress (X/Y completed)
- Daily streak counter
- Valuation Score (quality metric)
- Audio controls (mute/unmute)

## Stage Progression

### Complete Stages
- Finish all 4-5 checkpoints in a stage to advance
- Watch the world brighten:
  - Each completed stage adds 8.57% base brightness
  - Within a stage, each checkpoint adds up to 40% temporary brightness
  - Maximum brightness: 100% at final stage completion

### Encounter Bosses
- See your Super Boss silhouette in the distance (15% opacity)
- Boss becomes more visible as you progress:
  - Stage 5+: Present state (50% opacity)
  - Stage 7+: Foreground state (100% opacity)
- Encounter 8 mini-bosses at stage boundaries
- Hear unique boss entrance themes

## Audio Experience

### Immersive Soundscape
- Each biome has unique ambient music
- Smooth 800ms crossfades between biomes
- Checkpoint completion sound effects (different per animation pattern)
- Boss entrance themes
- Level-up fanfare
- Badge award sounds (different per rarity)
- UI interaction sounds (clicks, confirmations, errors)

### Audio Controls
- Adjust master volume
- Separate music and SFX volume controls
- Mute/unmute toggle in HUD
- Settings persist across sessions
- Compliant with browser autoplay policies

- Be thorough and complete (Completeness)

## Daily Engagement

### Build Your Streak
- Complete at least one checkpoint per day
- Track your streak in the HUD
- Maintain momentum for better progression

### Optimize Your Time
- Checkpoints take 10-30 minutes each
- Animations are skippable
- Progress saves automatically
- Return anytime to continue
